My First Impressions

When I first saw SP, I was shocked its size; the compound was extremely large! I explored SP during my first week and it was like going on an adventure.

There are a total of 6 Food Courts. Food Courts 4 and 6 are the closest to the School of Computing so I usually go there to eat. I recommend going to Food Court 4 as it's Koufu!

Every room I've been to is completely populated by sockets. I don't think it's possible to run out of battery haha!

The SPerience

The lessons here are quite similar to Secondary School. We just sit down and listen to the teacher or lecturer talk about the topic. I guess the main difference is that they care less about you? As in, you can quite literally walk out and nobody cares. Playing games in the middle of class? You're just disrupting your own learning. I get a weird gut feeling everytime I walk out because I'm not used to it yet üòÇ

theLocation

It's located right next to Dover MRT Station. Definitely not as close to my Secondary School but it's somewhat convenient. There's still quite a bit of walking to do regardless. I usually take the MRT because it's more consistent, whereas the bus route is not. But if the bus timing is good, I sometimes take the bus!
